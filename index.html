FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about DealGuardian.

What exactly is DealGuardian? ▼
DealGuardian is an AI‑powered deal intelligence platform that helps sales professionals generate pitches and analyze contracts to close deals faster and with more confidence.
How does the Pitch Generator work? ▼
You paste a company's 'About Us' text. Our AI extracts industry, revenue estimates, and pain points, then crafts a professional 3‑sentence pitch tailored to that specific company.
Is LegalEagle a replacement for legal counsel? ▼
No. LegalEagle is a risk‑awareness tool designed to highlight potential red flags. Always consult a qualified attorney for final contract review.
Do I need to create an account? ▼
Currently, no. Our demo is completely open. In future releases, accounts will be optional for saving history and team collaboration.
Is my data secure? ▼
Absolutely. We never store, log, or persist any text you paste. All processing is temporary and runs in memory. Your privacy is paramount.
