FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about DealGuardian AI.

What is DealGuardian AI? ▼
DealGuardian AI is a sales intelligence platform that uses AI to generate professional pitches and scan contracts for risks, helping you close deals faster.
How does the Pitch Generator work? ▼
Simply paste a company's 'About Us' text. Our AI extracts industry, revenue estimates, and pain points, then crafts a 3‑sentence professional pitch tailored to that company.
Is LegalEagle a substitute for a lawyer? ▼
No. LegalEagle is a risk‑awareness tool that helps you spot potential issues. Always consult a qualified legal professional for final contract review.
Do I need to create an account? ▼
Not yet! The demo version is completely open to use. In the future, we'll offer accounts for saving history and team collaboration.
Is my data secure? ▼
Absolutely. We do not store any text you paste. All processing is temporary and runs in memory. Your privacy is our priority.
